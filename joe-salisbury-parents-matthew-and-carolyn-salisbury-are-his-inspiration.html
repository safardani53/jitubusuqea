Joe Salisbury parents Matthew and Carolyn Salisbury, raised him in London, England, alongside his siblings, Katie and Chris. 

Willingly, his parents have maintained a private persona with little information available regarding their personal and professional life. Nonetheless, their life-long support in Joe’s life has not gone unseen.

His sister has a successful career outside of tennis, while his brother Chris shares his passion for tennis. 

Joe Salisbury, a former tennis world No. 1 in doubles, has rightfully earned acclaim for his remarkable abilities.  

The tennis player registered his maiden professional victory at the Shenzhen Open, claiming his first ATP Tour title. To this date, Joe has earned thirteen ATP Tour titles, including three at the Masters 1000 level. 

Further, Joe had a breakthrough season in 2021, securing three Grand Slam titles. With his victory at the US Open double and mixed, he became the first man since Bob Bryan to win both titles in the same year. 

Altogether with his incredible effort, Joe has bagged five Grand Slam titles. The player is still going strong, with fans hoping to see more from him.

Before turning pro, Joe previously played collegiate tennis at Memphis Tigers from 2010 to 2014, finishing with 97 doubles and 25 singles victories.

Joe Salisbury Parents: Matthew And Carolyn Salisbury

Matthew and his wife, Carolyn Salisbury, are the parents of the remarkable tennis player Joe Salisbury. Besides him, they have two kids named Chris and Katie Salisbury.

Joe’s parents have made considerable sacrifices, even if it meant spending countless hours driving him around for practice. Their persistent support helped him grow as a player transforming him into a world-class tennis player. 

Although Joe has been making waves worldwide, his parents, on the other hand, prefer to lead a low-key life. However, their assistance in shaping Joe’s career has certainly not gone unnoticed.

Also, his mother seems to be a track athlete, so it’s not surprising that the athletic gene runs in his family. Today, the player’s parents are undoubtedly proud of his success and the position he has reached today.  

In addition, Joe’s Instagram showcases his travel glimpses with his family; they recently traveled to Italy.
