Billy Ray Cyrus And Tish Cyrus Have Divorced Each Other For The Third Time

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  584 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Billy Ray Cyrus and his wife Tish Cyrus are no longer married. After more than 28 years of marriage, the couple is divorcing. This is the third time they’ve parted ways. Tish filed for divorce in Williamson County, Tennessee, on April 6, citing irreconcilable differences, according to court filings. They haven’t lived together in over two years, according to her. The spokespeople for the two have yet to comment.

Billy Ray Cyrus’ net worth

Since 1992, Billy Ray Cyrus has released 16 studio albums and 53 singles. He is most known for his track Achy Breaky Heart, which reached the top of the Hot Country Songs chart in the United States and was the first record to attain triple platinum status in Australia. The 60-year-net old’s worth is believed to be over $20 million, according to reports. Despite the fact that precise information on his assets is not public, he has amassed a substantial fortune as a singer, composer, and actor.

The Flying By singer is a multi-platinum selling musician with eight top-ten hits on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart. Some Gave All is one of his most successful albums to date, having been certified multi-platinum nine times in the United States. Some Gave All was in the top ten for 43 weeks, a record held by just one country album in history, Garth Brooks’ Ropin’ the Wind. It was the first debut album to debut at the top of Billboard’s Top Country Albums list. The record has sold over 20 million copies and is the best-selling debut album for a male solo artist of all time.

Billy has had 36 charting singles, with 17 of them reaching the top 40. In 2019, he received his first number-one record on the US Billboard Hot 100 as a featured artist on a remix of Lil Nas X’s song Old Town Road. He also won two Grammy Awards for the song, in the categories of Best Pop Duo/Group Performance and Best Music Video. From 2001 until 2004, he was a regular on the television program Doc. From 2006 to 2011, he co-starred in the Disney Channel comedy Hannah Montana with his daughter Miley Cyrus, and from 2016 to 2017, he portrayed Vernon Brownmule in the CMT sitcom Still the King.

Tish Cyrus and Billy Ray Cyrus’s relationship history

Tish Cyrus and Billy Ray Cyrus married in December 1993. The couple has five children: three girls, Miley, Noah, and Brandi, as well as two males, Trace and Braison. Billy first filed for divorce in 2010, but subsequently revealed that he had withdrawn his suit. In an interview, he stated:

“I’m no longer divorcing. I want to reassemble my family. “Things are better than they’ve ever been.”

However, things soured between the couple in 2013, and Tish filed for divorce this time, claiming that it is a personal problem and that they are striving to find a solution that is in the best interests of their family. The pair subsequently said that they were able to save their marriage via hard effort and couples counseling. In a statement, they said,

“We both awoke with the realization that we love one other and have chosen to remain together.” We both went to couples counseling, which we hadn’t done in 22 years, and it’s brought us closer together and opened up our communication in incredible ways.”
