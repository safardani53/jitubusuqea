Me and my husband have a date-abase to keep our relationship fun and its perfect if you can

 Posted on March 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  388 words  |  Aldo Pusey

WHILE we all want to spend more time with our partner - it can be tricky picking what to do.

Date night comes around, and we often spend hours figuring out how to spend quality time together, but not anymore.

Thankfully, Grace Booth took to social media to share her best relationship advice so you'll never be stumped on ideas again.

She said: "I feel like I should introduce you to.

One of my favourite things about my and my husband's relationship is that we have our date night spreadsheet."

Coining the term 'date-abase' - Grace revealed she has a spreadsheet full of fun date ideas so they never run out of plans.

The happily married couple has a mixture of dates to pick from, including pricey nights out to the theatre or a cosy night playing their favourite games.

Showing viewers one of her dates, Grace added: "Today I was showing you one of the dates that's on the spreadsheet, which is cheapest versus most expensive in a department store.

"It's so fun.

"You have to walk around and try and find the cheapest versus most expensive item."

The pair then brought their cheapest and most expensive finds to each other to see who had one.

And for those of us who are single, you can still participate in the fun.

The 'date-abase' is a great way to keep a running record of all the fun things you can do with your friends, family, or by yourself.

The clip posted to her TikTok account @gracebooth97 was clearly a hit with over 40k views and 2,500 likes.

Many were invested in seeing the date ideas Grace had on the spreadsheet and wanted to start their own.
