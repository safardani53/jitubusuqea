The View's Whoopi Goldberg has given co-host Sunny Hostin an NSFW birthday gift on live TV.

Whoopi, 66, suggested the treat could be something psychedelic as the star hinted the present will make her happy later.

The View hosts celebrated Sunny's 54th birthday during Thursday's broadcast of the morning talk show.

Near the end of the episode, after The Isley Brothers performed an electrifying birthday tribute, the panel was joined by WABC news anchors Joe Torres and David Navarro.

The pair of anchors rolled out a cart that carried dishes from Sunny's favorite restaurant, Bronx, New York staple, Barrio BX.

After introducing several of the dishes, including pollo guisado and flan, Joe Torres stated "We have everything."

Whoopi quickly interrupts, voicing "You don't have everything," as she presents Sunny with a rainbow-colored gift bag.

She continues, "This was made homemade for you," and gives Sunny a knowing glance as she begins to hand off the package.

Apparently changing her mind, the EGOT winner pulls back the bag as she explains "I'm not going to tell you what it is. I'll give it to you later."

Sunny's eyes widen as she exclaims "Ohhh," turning back toward the audience seated in front of the stage.

David Navarro chimes, "It's for the holidays," before Whoopi comments "It'll make you happy."

Sunny makes a drinking motion with her hand as the 66-year-old host again interjects, "You can't have it now," seemingly inferring that it could be an edible.

Before the sign-off, Whoopi places the bag into Sunny hands and states: "You can hold it. He couldn't give it to you...I can."

ARE YOU OKAY?

Yesterday, Whoopi and Sunny had a much different interaction when Whoopi savagely called out her co-host on live TV.

On Tuesday's show during Hot Topics, the women discussed whether people were paying attention or not to what is going on.

"I think you have to have a little more faith in people and they have made up their minds for a reason," Whoopi said, referring to the upcoming elections.

As she continued to talk, the camera stayed on her, but she called out Sunny, who appeared to not be listening.

"Why are you looking at me like that?" The View host asked.

The camera panned to Sunny, who had a pout on her face and was looking down.

When the room got quiet, she looked up and asked: "Oh, who me?"

"Yeah," Whoopi replied.

"We lost Sunny," Alyssa Farah Griffin laughed.

"Yeah. Sometimes we get brain farts on the show. I just had one. Sorry everyone," Sunny admitted.

"No. No. I looked over and she was like...." Whoopi proceeded to mock her facial expression. "And I thought, 'she looks like I do sometimes, but let me ask her if she's ok.'"

"Brain fart.... on live television. Thanks for pointing that out Whoopi," Sunny chuckled.

"Well, Sunny I didn't know what was happening and I wanted to check," Whoopi informed her.

PHONE FOR SUNNY

However, this isn't the only awkward moment Sunny experienced this week on TV.

During a heated discussion with the panelists, she interrupted the conversation as Whoopi and Alyssa butted heads over their political views.

In the midst of the battle, Sunny's cell phone began to ring, directing Whoopi's attention to the device sitting on the table.

"Hello, hello, hello," the 66-year-old moderator commented, looking at Sunny.

"See who it is," Sunny pointed her screen at Whoopi, seated at the other end of the panel.

"Who is it? You know I can't read -" the actress remarked, noting the phone's distance.

"Gabriel Hostin," the 54-year-old replied, reading her son's name on the screen.
