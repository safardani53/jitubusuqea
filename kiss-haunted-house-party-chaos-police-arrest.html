 Posted on July 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  427 words  |  Aldo Pusey

THE KISS Haunted House Party at Wembley has ended in chaos tonight with mass disorder and "numerous" arrests.

Police officers from three different forces have swarmed Wembley's SSE arena in London, where Stormzy and Liam Payne were performing tonight.

Photos and footage from the scene show dozens of party goers standing outside, as police cars continue to drive in.

McDonald's on Wembley Road has been forced to close as the mayhem spilled out into the surrounding around.

Did you see the disorder in Wembley? If you have pictures or video from the scene please email us at tips-sm@news.co.uk

Brent Police said on Twitter: "Officers are on scene at #WembleyArea following significant disorder after the #KissHauntedHouse event.

"Numerous arrests have been made, officers are asking people to disperse immediately to the nearby #WembleyPark & #WembleyCentral Tube Stations."

Cops are expected to remain around the arena until the disorder settles.

The Sun Online understands arrests were made in response to disorderly behaviour.

More than 12,000 fans gathered to see their favourite pop stars perform tonight for the special Halloween bash.

Fans enjoyed more than three hours of music in what might be the biggest Halloween party in the city this year.

Celebs also flocked in fancy dress to the party wearing their spookiest costumes for the gig.

Stormzy wore a sinister golden mask with a tattered khaki jacket and black combat jeans during his performance.

A spokesperson for KISS Haunted House Party said: “The safety of event goers is of huge priority to us. We have cooperated fully with the venue and police to ensure a swift resolve to any issues this evening.”
