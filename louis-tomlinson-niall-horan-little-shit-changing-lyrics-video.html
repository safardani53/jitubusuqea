Louis and Liam spent their time and energy crafting lyrics to “Change Your Ticket,” and it was Niall who surprised them both by changing them! Louis and Liam personally liked the lyrics they had written, but Niall had other plans. He smiled and reminded Louis that he was the one who changed them, and that’s when Louis called him a “little sh*t!”

“I heard people didn’t like it, so I went with it,” Niall explained. Sometimes you gotta do what you gotta do! The boys laughed and joked around — there’s clearly no bad blood over the lyrics change.

The boys are gearing up for a long break, and Louis revealed on the show that the hiatus will last 18 months! Before you start freaking out, the boys have stressed that they will be back. This break is not forever.
