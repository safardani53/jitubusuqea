IF you are looking to bag yourself some sweet treats for Christmas, you’ve come to the right place.

An M&S super fan has shared the new festive items you’ll want to nab from the high street chain, including a chocolate game that’s sure to be a family favourite.

With prices starting at 75p, there’s a range of great snacks, as well as sweet tins that’ll make perfect gifts.

So if you love chocolate and sweets, or know someone who does, you’ll want to check this out.

Ashleigh, a money-saving whizz, took to social media to show off the chain’s festive offerings, as she shared her video with the caption “Christmas yummies have landed in M&S Food and I think I need one of everything!”

She then gave her followers a close-up look at the treats and said: “Guys, get yourself to Marks & Spencer because there's even more Christmas food and snacks in store now.

“This four in a row chocolate game looks like it's gonna be a firm family favourite for the festive season.

“There's also the original Very Merry Munch boxes. These are £9.

“They are kind of like sweet trail mixes, but they're so yummy. I love these, I'm a big fan. 

“There's also this Percy Pig pig ‘n’ mix. This is £5 with sweets and chocolate inside.

“And then there's also a Colin the Caterpillar version as well, so if you wanna mix up your chocolate and sweets, you can grab these for the kids, or for yourself.”

If you want to give someone a sweet gift this December, Ashleigh continued: “There's this Percy Pig tin filled with goodies. This is £5. This will make a great gift.

“And there's also this paint by numbers mini chocolate chip cookie house tin as well.

“For £2 you can pick up this Christmas veg bag of mini chocolates, which looks cute.”

There’s also really affordable treats available too, as Ashleigh explained: “Bubbly Santas, can't wait to try. And also a caramel tree for 75p.

“This themed box of seasonal vegetables are actually chocolates, for £3.50. These are really cute as well.

“And there's also these puppies for £3, made of chocolate.

“And Spencer Bear for £3 as well.

“Percy Pig is also going festive for Christmas. We've got the Festive Helpers bag and then there's also these, the Merry Percy Miss bag.

"So cute! Perfect if you love Percy Pigs.

“And there's a Colin the Caterpillar candy cane sweet as well, so get yourself to M&S.” 

The TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @ashleighmoneysaver, has clearly left many stunned, as it has quickly amassed 60,100 views.

Social media users were stunned at the new buys - but many took to the comments to express the same complaints. 

One person said: “It's over priced this year. Very disappointed with the actual products and sizing. You're paying for the designs.”

Another added: “Cost of living crisis and you want people to buy chocolate connect 4?!”

A third commented: “It's overpriced. I brought my own things to make a merry mix from Home Bargains.”
