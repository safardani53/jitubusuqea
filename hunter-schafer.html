Hunter Schafer Biography

Born In Raleigh, North Carlina

Hunter Schafer was born on 31st December 1999, in Raleigh, North Carolina, United States. She is one of four kids of pastor father Mac Schafer and mother, Katy Schafer. She holds the American nationality and belongs to white ethnicity.

She was raised along with her two sisters and a brother in her hometown in North Carolina.

She attended Needham B. Broughton High School. Later transferred to the University of North Carolina School of Arts and graduated with a major in Visual Arts Program in 2017.

Before Debut In Television She Started As A Model

Hunter also granted a chance to interview with former United States Senator and Secretary of State Hillary Clinton.
