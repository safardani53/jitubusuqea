NewJeanss Minji Shares Her Experience As A Chanel Brand Ambassador

 Posted on February 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  460 words  |  Martina Birk

Minji, the popular star of rookie K-pop girl group NewJeans, has reached a personal milestone by becoming the newest worldwide brand ambassador of one of the world’s most recognized luxury fashion companies, Chanel. The artist just did her first picture session for the firm. Along with the photo shoot, she spoke with the renowned fashion magazine Elle.

Minji discussed her career development and recalled her earlier photo shoot with the magazine, which appeared in the September 2022 issue. Minji of NewJeans recalled that she appreciated looking back at her prior pictorials since they gave her wonderful memories associated with those times. The idol traced her path and reflected on the many fashions she had worn. She said, ”

“It’s exciting to see various aspects of myself represented in movies or images. I believe that when I return to look at them once more time has gone, all of these things will be even nicer recollections.”

“I consider myself to be a lucky person,” says NewJeans Minji of her personality and more.

On Valentine’s Day, NewsJeans shared an Instagram photo highlighting Minji’s momentous effort in collaboration with Chanel. Minji is shown wearing a particular edition of the brand’s jacket, as well as a distinctive cap. The light makeup done with a broad selection of Chanel beauty products adds to her glam, making her appear even more lovely. Speaking about this chance and her overall luck, the group’s 18-year-old idol said that she feels lucky since she has been fortunate with the opportunities she has had. She also said that she does not hesitate to grasp such excellent opportunities. She stated:

“I consider myself to be someone who has wonderful fortune. A fortunate chance presented itself to me early on, and whenever such possibilities presented themselves to me, I attempted to grasp them.”

Regarding the evaluations, NewJeans’ Minji offered the comments that made her feel most accomplished. According to the idol, hearing from the crowd that they completely appreciated the concert makes her very happy. It’s a fantastic motivator for her to keep working hard to delight her fans, she says. She elaborated, saying:

“The answer I appreciate hearing the most from people who have seen us play is that they felt delighted the whole time they were watching us. It makes me pleased that we were able to transmit our excitement while still having fun on stage.”

This past month, NewJeans’ Hanni was selected to become the international cosmetics spokesperson for Armani Beauty. Hanni now joins the ranks of other renowned superstars in this coveted profession, including Cate Blanchett, Barbara Palvin, and Tessa Thompson. Hyein is also the face of Louis Vuitton, while Danielle is the face of Burberry. Fans are excited to discover which premium brand NewJeans’ Haerin will represent.
