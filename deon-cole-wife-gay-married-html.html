From a successful career as a comedian, Dean Cole has garnished a net worth of $500 thousand.

He began performing stand-up in the Chicago area, writing comedy for television in 1993, working on "Comic Justice." Subsequently, he went on to write for shows such as Def Comedy Jam, The Funny Spot, and 1st Amendment Stand Up.
