Demi Moores children - how many does she have with Bruce Willis?

 Posted on September 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  633 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

DEMI Moore was famously once married to Hollywood megastar, Bruce Willis.

They also amassed quite a brood during their time together - here's the lowdown.

How many children does Demi Moore have with Bruce Willis?

Demi Moore, 59, and Bruce Willis, 67, have three children together - who have also had a taste of fame.

So what do we know about them?

Here's the lowdown on their star kids:

Rumer Willis

33-year-old Rumer is probably the most famous out of her sisters.

She is Demi and Bruce's eldest daughter.

Rumer has made quite a name for herself on TV and starred in teen drama 9o210 and also Empire.

In 2015, she won Dancing with the Stars and then went on to make her Broadway debut in Chicago as Roxie Hart that same year.

Scout Willis

Middle child Scout, 30, was named after a character in the famous novel, To Kill A Mockingbird.

Not only has she worked in costume and wardrobe departments for several movies, but she has also acted in films like Bandits, Breakfast of Champions, and The Scarlet Letter.

Scout is an outspoken activist for women’s rights.

Tallulah Willis

28-year-old Tallulah is an actress, fashion designer as well as an entrepreneur.

Having had success in the early part of her life as an actress, she later got into designing and makeup and has now been working on her own business.

Like her mother, she has struggled with alcohol in the past.

In 2017, Tallulah posted a caption on Instagram, talking about her newfound sobriety.

In her post, she said she “did not value” herself or her body and self-medicated.

When was Demi Moore married to Bruce Willis?

Demi has been married three times during her life.

Her second marriage was to Bruce, which began on November 21, 1987, and ended on October 18, 2000.

In her explosive 2019 autobiography Inside Out she claims that their whirlwind romance saw them dating, married and pregnant all within four months.

She also says that he wanted her out of Hollywood movies and at home with their children.

Is Bruce Willis quitting acting?

Bruce is quitting Hollywood after he was diagnosed with aphasia, according to his daughter, Rumer.

She broke the news on March 30, 2022, via Instagram:

"To Bruce’s amazing supporters, as a family we wanted to share that our beloved Bruce has been experiencing some health issues and has recently been diagnosed with aphasia, which is impacting his cognitive abilities.

"As a result of this and with much consideration Bruce is stepping away from the career that has meant so much to him.

"This is a really challenging time for our family and we are so appreciative of your continued love, compassion and support.

"We are moving through this as a strong family unit, and wanted to bring his fans in because we know how much he means to you, as you do to him.

"As Bruce always says, 'Live it up' and together we plan to do just that," she concluded.

Did Demi Moore have a miscarriage when she was married to Ashton Kutcher?

The former married couple, who were together for 10 years after meeting in 2003, suffered a devastating miscarriage at the six-month mark.

Following the release of her explosive book, Demi revealed a previously unseen photograph of her huge baby bump.

Demi, who was 42 at the time, was six months pregnant when she lost the baby.

She recalled the doctors' words as she discovered their child had passed away and told the host: "You could see combination of his dread and it shifting then immediately in to matter of fact, practical, information.

"Because it was unquestionable. There was no heartbeat."
