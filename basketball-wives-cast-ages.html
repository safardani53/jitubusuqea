'Basketball Wives' Season 11 stars Brooke Bailey, Evelyn Lozada, Jac'Eil Duckworth, Jackie Christie, and others. How old are these women?

As the women make it a point to flaunt their luxurious lifestyles and connections, you may find yourself wondering about the ages of the cast. After all, some women have been married for decades, while others have been divorced for some time. Keep reading to get the lowdown on everyone's age.

Jackie Christie (Age 53)

It's only right we start with the OG. Jackie Christie! According to FamousBirthdays, Jackie's born day is July 25, 1969, which makes her 53 years old during Season 11. The housewife is married to former NBA star and coach Doug Christie. Over the years, Jackie has showcased the couple's vow renewals on the series and has tackled several family issues.

Jennifer Williams (Age 49)

Jennifer, who is the founder of The Lucid Cosmetics, Flirty Girl Fitness, and Classy Girl Wardrobe is currently 49 years old. Once married to former basketball player, Eric Williams, has been an integral part of the Basketball Wives cast from the beginning. She is currently engaged to Christian Gold as of Aug. 27, 2023.

Brooke Bailey (Age 46)

Brooke Bailey's birthday is July 13, 1977, making her a Cancer. The gorgeous video vixen, model, and entrepreneur made her return to Basketball Wives after departing from the LA franchise in Season 2.

Jac'Eil Duckworth (Age 36)

Jac'Eil Duckworth (real name Yeisha Jaceil Duckworth) was born and raised in a small Mississippi town. Her age is estimated to be 36 years old with her being born in 1988, per a source. A self-proclaimed “hair genius," Jac'Eil's business is a huge priority, but it comes second to raising her son Prince Raylon Dy'Khi Drummond, and building a life with her fiancée, WNBA superstar Natasha Howard.

Vanessa Rider (Age 42)

Vanessa Rider is the wife of veteran NBA champion, and slam dunk champion Isaiah Rider. Born in Phoenix, Ariz. on July 15, 1981, Vanessa is currently 42 years old. She is also a mother of four, an established businesswoman, and the definition of a boss babe.

Evelyn Lozada (Age 48)

Evelyn Lozada is a franchise favorite. Born in the Bronx, NY on December 10, 1975, Evelyn is currently 48 years old. The influencer, yoga instructor, and mom to daughter Shaniece Hairston, also runs her namesake non-profit The Evelyn Lozada Foundation, which focuses on empowering women and girls as well as providing support to individuals who have suffered domestic violence. She is currently engaged to LaVon Lewis, who she met on the reality series Queens Court.
