SHOPPERS are racing to grab a bargain find on the high-street.

We'd all love the chance to get some designer goodies for free, and now we finally can thanks to Superdrug.

The beauty and health store has savvy shoppers in a frenzy thanks to their latest offer.

One shopper noticed and couldn't help but share the news on Facebook.

It seems that Superdrug is offering a free Moschino handbag with every purchase of the designer's perfume, Uomo.

Taking to the group Extreme Couponing And Bargains UK, Amy Riply shared her bargain find.

"Really think it's a good offer so worth getting!"

The bag comes in a black satin material with handles and is covered in a print of shiny jewels and features the metal Moschino logo.

Beauty fans are also going wild over Drunk Elephant tanning drop dupes that can be found in store.

Not only that, but beauty fans have been calling the bargain buy a dupe for the popular 'Drunk Elephant D-Bronzi Anti-Pollution Sun Drops,' which will set you back a whopping £31.

The 'Studio London U DEW U Bronzing Drops,' which are available in two different shades - light/medium and medium/dark - are an impressive £26 cheaper when compared to the cult brand.

Both products offer the same bronzed look and simply need to be added into a moisturiser or foundation before being applied to the face.
