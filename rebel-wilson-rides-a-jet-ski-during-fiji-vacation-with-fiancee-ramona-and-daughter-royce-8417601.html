Rebel Wilson Rides a Jet Ski During Fiji Vacation

 Posted on July 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  530 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Rebel Wilson is living life to the fullest.

The Pitch Perfect actress shared some photos on her Instagram from her romantic getaway to Fiji with her fiancée Ramona Agruma.

On Saturday, Wilson, 43, shared two photos of herself riding a jet ski in the crystal clear island waters, and she wrote in the caption, “I ❤️ coming to Fiji so so much!”

She wore a black swimsuit top and shorts with sunglasses and a pink New York Yankees hat as she posed on the black-and-yellow jet ski, which she was operating herself. 

Her fiancée shared some love on the post, commenting “❤️❤️❤️.”

Based on another one of her posts from the trip, the couple are staying on Laucala island, a privately owned resort island.

Wilson shared another shot of herself on the beach in a low-cut bright orange swimsuit. She held a tropical coconut drink in her hand and captioned the photo, “Find me in Fiji.”

In a short clip on her Instagram Story, she shared a video of herself and Agruma chilling on beach chairs in the sun as she said of the moment, “Total relaxation.”

The couple have been enjoying their island getaway, and brought their 1-year-old daughter, Royce Lillian, along with them for the trip. 

Agruma shared a sweet photo of the toddler posing in a pink shirt and matching pink sunglasses, which she tagged with the location as Fiji. “Modeling 😍💗,” she captioned the shot of her daughter.

The family trip to Fiji comes just weeks after the couple celebrated their daughter’s first birthday with an extravagant — and pink-themed — party. 

In posts on Instagram, both Wilson and Agruma showed off the celebration, which was full of cake, balloons and laughter. 

There were tables full of treats — including a mermaid-themed cake — and decorations, and Royce sat in a chair with a crop of pink balloons behind her and a pink outfit on as she celebrated her first birthday. 

She looked adorable in the sweet shots shared by her parents, and Wilson said ““Happy birthday, Roycieeee!” in one video as her daughter squealed and clapped.

Wilson and Agruma welcomed Royce via surrogate in November 2022, and announced the news on Instagram, surprising the world. 

The actress said having a daughter had “been years in the making” and called her “the BEST gift!!”

"I can't even describe the love I have for her, she's a beautiful miracle!” Wilson said of her newborn.
