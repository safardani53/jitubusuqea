THIS is the shocking moment a car ploughed into a crowd of drinkers outside a leafy pub in a hit-and-run.

The group were stood in the car park of the The Bear on the Barge pub in the North West London suburb of Harefield when the horror unfolded.

Terrifying footage shows the car speeding as it approaches the group.

It then sends drinkers flying as it slams into them - leaving some lying on the ground.

As screaming passers-by rush over to the help, the car reverses before driving away.

One drinker can be seen trying to chase the motor but he falls over as it races off.

A second clip shows a man being kicked on the ground but it is not clear if this is related.

Two people were rushed to hospital after the hit-and-run at around 1.20am.

They were treated for non-life threatening injuries.

The Met Police confirmed there had been no arrests and they are now appealing for witnesses.

They said: "Police were called at 01:18hrs on Saturday, 1 May to reports of a collision at Moorhall Road, Uxbridge.
