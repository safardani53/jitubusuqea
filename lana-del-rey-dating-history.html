Lana Del Rey dating history: Who has the Margaret singer dated?

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  10 minutes  |  2034 words  |  Aldo Pusey

THE iconic star shocked fans when she revealed that she was single again in November 2023.

Lana Del Rey had previously announced her engagement to musician and music manager, Evan Winiker, in March 2023.

Who has Lana Del Rey dated?

Born Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, the Let Me Love You Like a Woman singer has had her fair share of past relationships.

The American icon's music is known for exploring themes of love, romance, tragedy, and melancholia, which may be inspired by her own intense, tumultuous love life.

Check out Lana Del Rey's complete dating history, including casual flings, ex-boyfriends, and ex-fiancées, below.

Barrie-James O'Neill

Lana began dating Scottish singer-songwriter Barrie-James O'Neill in 2011.

The duo began writing and recording music together, and O'Neill was one of the few people the songstress worked with in her early years.

They made their red carpet debut at the MTV Europe Music Awards in 2012.

In a 2012 interview with Just Jared, Lana revealed that many of her songs were inspired by O'Neill, stating: "We always say that the entire time we were writing for each other, but we didn't know it until we met each other."

O'Neill's band, Kassidy, even joined the singer on her Paradise tour in 2013.

That same year, E! News reported that the celebrity couple might even be engaged after spotting Lana sporting a diamond on her ring finger.

While their engagement was never confirmed, the duo had broken up by 2014.

Lana Del Rey told the German-language Swiss newspaper 20 Minuten that while O'Neill was "a wonderful person....there are some things he has to deal with."

She added: "This was hard on our relationship. I no longer felt free."

O'Neill later revealed that he found out that Lana had moved on from the media.

As reported by New Musical Express, O'Neill told the Scottish Sun that he wasn't aware of the breakup until his cousin showed him a news article about the split.

O'Neill, who was struggling with depression and insomnia at the time, said: "I felt like a burden on Lana – as she was having to take care of me."

Despite calling it quits romantically, the duo still make music together years later.

In 2021, O'Neill helped write several tracks on Lana's eighth studio album, Blue Bannisters.

Francesco Carrozzini

Shortly after splitting up with O'Neill, Lana was spotted with photographer Francesco Carrozzini.

Carrozzini was the son of Vogue Italia's late editor-in-chief, Franca Sozzani.

The couple never publicly confirmed their relationship, though they couldn't escape the paparazzi's obsession with their relationship.

They were frequently photographed together in Portofino at the start of their romance.

The duo also packed on the PDA while watching a World Cup game.

In 2015, Carrozzini was also spotted celebrating the New Year with the Cinnamon Girl singer in St Barts, a popular celebrity hotspot.

The couple were last spotted together in September 2015, and by November, they had split up for good.

G-Eazy

Lana was first spotted hanging out with G-Eazy at Coachella in April 2017.

The duo were photographed leaving a Hollywood nightclub together less than a month later.

The pair seemed to have cooled off almost immediately, as G-Eazy started dating New Americana singer Halsey that summer.

In 2018, Lana reportedly shaded her brief fling during a live performance of her song White Mustang.

She changed the original lyrics, "Couldn't stop the way I was feeling the day your record dropped" to "Couldn't stop the way I was feeling the day his record dropped and it wasn't even that good."

Two years later, the rapper reportedly fired back at the legendary singer, saying she had tried to get back together with him.

In his 2020 song, Moana, G-Eazy rapped: "I know a Keisha and I know Lana / They try to get me back, but I'm like, 'No, nah, nah."

Sean Larkin

Fans rejoiced when Lana was linked to a real-life cop Sean "Sticks" Larkin in 2019.

Larkin is also a famous TV personality, regularly appearing on Live PD as an analyst.

He told People magazine that the duo, who met in New York City, "just kind of hit it off from day one."

The couple split their time between Los Angeles, Lana's home, and his home in Tulsa, Oklahoma.

Larkin added that his two daughters, who were in their late teens and early 20s at the time, "were kind of blown away" hearing about "dad's new girlfriend."

Lana confirmed their romance in October 2019, telling People magazine that Larkin was a "good cop," and that she "would've worn something different" had she known photos of the pair walking in Central Park would have gone viral.

In December 2019, the duo went official on Instagram after Lana posted a photo of the two embracing and kissing.

They even attended a pre-Grammys gala together in January 2020, making their red-carpet debut.

By March 2020, the relationship had fizzled out.

In a profile by the New York Times titled Policeman. Reality Star. Recent Ex-Boyfriend, Larkin said: “Right now, we’re just friends."

"We still talk and whatnot, we just have busy schedules right now.”

According to Lana, the breakup wasn't as amicable as Larkin made it seem.

She announced the release of her album, Did You Know There's a Tunnel Under Ocean Boulevard, by placing a billboard advertising the album in Tulsa, Larkin's hometown.

She revealed the billboard – and the album – on Larkin's birthday in December 2022.

In a now-deleted social media post, she wrote: "There's only one [billboard] and it's in Tulsa."

She also left a message in the photo's comment section, writing: "It's. Personal."

Clayton Johnson

Following her breakup with Larkin, Lana soon found solace in her relationship with musician Clayton Johnson.

In August 2020, Lana and Johnson started following each other on social media.

The duo had reportedly met via the dating app, Bumble.

Lana first posted her singer beau in an Instagram story in September 2020.

Eagle-eyed fans quickly researched the singer and found Johnson's old tweets, where he expressed support for Donald Trump and regularly used derogatory language.

On September 8, 2020, Johnson apologized via X, formerly Twitter.

He wrote: "Yesterday, tweets from my teenage years 12 years ago were dug up."

"While age is not an excuse for any derogatory language, I’m thankful to have grown and learned over the years from friends, family, and my community."

He added: "I did not vote for [Trump]. I don't think being ignorant in the past makes me racist. Education and action are most important."

In December 2020, Lana spent the holidays with Johnson's family at his parents' farm in Modesto, California.

During their relationship, Lana was spotted wearing a diamond ring in multiple photos and outings, but the couple never confirmed any engagement rumors.

Johnson also worked with the singer on her album, Blue Bannisters.

By February 2021, Johnson and Lana were Instagram official after he shared a photo of the duo on his birthday.

It's unclear when the two broke things off, though by early 2022, Lana had her sights set on a new man.

Jack Donoghue

Jack Donoghue and Lana Del Rey first sparked relationship rumors when Donoghue posted a now-deleted video of the pair attending a monster truck race in early 2022.

Lana was also photographed with Donoghue at his birthday celebration in May 2022.

However, the duo truly broke the internet when they hard-launched their relationship on July 12, 2022.

The couple posed for a photo outside of Chicago's Cook County Jail.

Donoghue captioned the post: "Family visit," indicating that the pair were visiting one of his family members inside the prison.

Donoghue is one-half of the band Salem, where he collaborates with fellow musician John Holland.

He also is a talented music producer, working on Kanye West's Yeezus album and creating remixes for Charli XCX.

He was previously linked to artist and musician Courtney Love, and reportedly counts actress, author, and fashion icon Julia Fox as a friend.

A source reportedly told the Daily Mail that: "Jack is really romantic."

"He’s totally crazy about Lana. While she was away for a few days, he was playing her songs really loud."

In February 2023, fans of the couple were able to spot a photo of the same bouquet of flowers on both Donoghue and Lana's Instagram Stories.

Lana had received the flowers around Valentine's Day, and captioned the photo: "It's a love-hate thing."

The hate must have won over love, as the singer revealed her engagement to Evan Winiker less than two months later.

Evan Winiker

Evan Winiker made headlines for his relationship with Lana Del Rey, but he's also known for his successful managerial career in the music industry.

In March 2021, he joined the Range Media team as a managing partner and now works alongside the likes of MAX, Daya, Disco Biscuits, Skyler Stonestreet, and Walk Off the Earth.

“As a manager, it’s always my goal to surround my clients with the best teams in all areas,” he told Variety.

“This ecosystem is truly that. It’s an environment of forward-thinking people who look beyond the boundaries and walls that are categorically put in place, allowing them to open new doors and reach new peaks that we dream up.”

Prior to Range Media, he worked at Full Stop Management and was a member of the indie rock band Steel Train, according to Billboard.

Lana and Evan's engagement was first reported by Billboard on March 30, 2023, and came just months after they were spotted out in public together.

While their engagement might have been confirmed, details of their relationship are unclear.

It's unknown when they met, or when they officially started dating.

The announcement came amid an exciting year for Lana, who just released her ninth studio album, Did You Know There’s a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd, and was honored with the Visionary Award at Billboard's 2023 Women in Music ceremony.

“I feel like being happy is the ultimate goal. So I did it,” she said during her acceptance speech.

Unfortunately, the engagement was short-lived.

In her December cover story for Harper's Bazaar, the singer revealed that her engagement – and relationship – with Winiker was over.

She said: “In that person’s case, there was something going on with them, like a little bubble ego."

The Summertime Sadness singer said her Los Angeles home was actually a point of contention for her most recent ex and for her past relationships.

“I feel like even the most chill guy doesn’t really want to chill here,” she said, referencing her home.

“Sadly, part of you knows...that ain’t it...that one shocked me.”

Beyond using her "simple" house as an "accidental" compatibility test for her partners, Lana also added that her ex preferred the "idea of her" versus who she actually is.

As for what the future holds?

The Ride singer says she's comfortable being single, but always open to the possibility of a new romance.

She tells Harper's Bazaar, "I’m learning. I know what I want."

Does Lana Del Rey have kids?

Lana first rose to fame in the early 2010s following the release of her debut single, Video Games.

Since then, she has become one of the most iconic singers in the industry, but she is known for keeping details of her personal life out of the spotlight.

While not much is known about her family life, she does not currently have any children.

It is also unknown if she is planning on having any kids in the future.

However, that doesn't stop the Art Deco singer from appreciating motherhood.

As reported by Dazed Digital, on May 10, 2020, Lana posted a heartwarming, honest tribute to Mother's Day, while also sharing a carousel of photos of herself and her mother, Pat Grant.

She added: "I am the way I am because of the women along the way who have taught me everything I needed to know and loved me unconditionally."
