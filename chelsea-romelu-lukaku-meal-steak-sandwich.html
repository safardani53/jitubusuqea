Chelsea stars including Romelu Lukaku celebrate Club World Cup win with plush meal where it's 65 f

 Posted on June 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  264 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

CHELSEA stars toasted their Club World Cup success last night with a swanky Knightsbridge meal.

A number of Thomas Tuchel's squad headed to plush Japanese-Italian restaurant Sumosan Twiga as they enjoyed a Tuesday night off.

The Blues won the first Club World Cup title in their history on Saturday by beating Brazilian side Palmeiras 2-1 after extra time in Abu Dhabi.

They've since jetted home but with no game this mid-week, opted to engage in some team bonding.

Players headed to Sumosan Twiga to let their hair down and enjoy some quality food.

A steak sandwich from the West London hotspot sets punters back £65, while starters such as a leg of crab can cost as much as £80.

As nights progress at Sumosan Twiga, tables get cleared and a DJ turns up the tunes.

The like of Romelu Lukaku, Cesar Azpilicueta and N'Golo Kante were pictured exiting the venue last night.

While Edouard Mendy, Marcos Alonso, Hakim Ziyech, Jorginho, Mateo Kovacic and Trevoh Chalobah were also snapped.

Antonio Rudiger might be heavily linked with a move away, but that hasn't stopped him engaging in team bonding activities such as this.

Meanwhile, injured duo Reece James and Ben Chilwell were also able to join in.

The Blues will be back in action this weekend, when they travel to Crystal Palace on Saturday afternoon.

A Champions League date with Lille then follows, as Tuchel and Co continue their trophy defence.
