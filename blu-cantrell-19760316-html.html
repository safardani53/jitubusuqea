Blu Cantrell is an American singer and songwriter of R&B and soul. Cantrell rose to fame in 2001, with the release of his first single, "Hit 'Em Up Style," reaching number two on the US Billboard Hot 100 and topping the US Mainstream Top 40 chart. The song was also recorded in several other countries and was included in her debut album, So Blu. The song earned Cantrell a Grammy nomination. In 2003 Cantrell released his second album, Bittersweet, which was nominated for a Grammy Award and the result was the single “Breathe”. Written and produced by Ivan Matias, “Breathe” was a huge worldwide hit in 2003, particularly in the UK, where it topped the UK Singles Chart for four consecutive weeks. “Breathe” also entered the top 10 on several other global charts, including the European Hot 100.
