Xavi Simons | Early Life & Career

Xavi Simons was born in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, on April 21, 2003. Ever since Simons learned how to walk, he also started playing soccer.

The main reason is that his father, Rogelio, was an ex-soccer player. Additionally, he also coached the youth team of Ajax.

Hence, Xavi dreamed of becoming a professional player like his old man. After that, be it day or night, the Amsterdam native spent his time honing and developing his skills.

Even at such a young age, everyone could see that Simons was an exceptional talent.

Thus, at the tender age of six, Barcelona wanted to train the talented kid at their famed La Masia academy, which had produced the likes of Messi and Iniesta, to name a few.

After that, Xavi rose through the ranks performing excellently at every youth level. As a result, the 20-year-old was handed the captaincy of the youth team.

Furthermore, while at La Masia, Simons won many teams and individual awards that propelled his status as a future great.

Xavi Simons | Career (PSG & Barcelona)

In 2019, Xavi’s only real competitive appearance was in the UEFA Youth League during the 2019-20 season.

Simons is not the product of Paris’ youth academy. Instead, he is a result of Barcelona’s world-renowned La Masia academy.

As a matter of fact, the 20-year-old spent nearly a decade with the Blaugrana before deciding to sign for Paris St. Germain in 2019.

Paris Saint Germain

In July 2019, Simons moved to French side PSG, neglecting to concur to another agreement with Barcelona.

His three-year deal with the Parisian club was worth up to €1 million ($1.04 million) per year. Simons made his professional debut for PSG on February 10, 2021, when he came as a substitute for Julian Draxler in a 1–0 Coupe de France victory over Caen.

Two months later, he won the Coupe de France, his first professional trophy, on May 19, 2021.

Manager Mauricio Pochettino included Simons in the first-team preparation and preseason before the 2021–22 season.

On 14 July 2021, he came off the seat and scored a goal in a 4-0 cordial win over Le Monitors at the Camp des Loges. Three days later, at the same stadium, he scored another goal in a 2–2 friendly draw against Chambly.

On 18 August, Simons was sent back to prepare with the under-19 crew, instructed by Zoumana Camara.

He eventually made his first professional start for PSG on December 19, 2021, making his first appearance of the season in a 3–0 cup victory over Feignies Aulnoye.

Simons passed Kylian Mbappé for his first senior assist on January 3, 2022, in a 4–0 cup victory over Vannes. On January 9, 2022, he made his first league appearance of the 2021–22 season, making a 69th-minute substitution in a 1–1 home draw against Lyon.

In the Coupe de France round of 16 on January 31, Simons missed the final penalty in a 6–5 shootout loss to Nice. He made his debut in the league in a 1–0 victory over Rennes on February 11.

PSV

PSV Simons signed a five-year contract with the Eredivisie club PSV on June 28, 2022.
Even though he initially anticipated extending his PSG contract and joining PSV on loan.

However, a buy-back clause in Simons’ contract that takes effect in 2023 was negotiated by PSG.

On July 30, 2022, Simons made his debut for the PSV in a 5–3 victory over rivals Ajax in the Johan Cruyff Shield.

On August 7, 2022, Simons made his debut in the Eredivisie game by assisting Johan Bakayoko.

International

Because Xavi has not made any first-team appearance for his club team, it would have been a shock had the 20-year-old already played for the famed Dutch senior national team.

However, there will not be any surprises today because Simons has only appeared for the junior side of his national side.

The Amsterdam native played in the U-17 World Cup in 2019 and the UEFA European U-17 Championship qualification.

Simons joined the preselection of the Dutch national team for the first time, On 21 October 2022.

Later, he was eventually called up for the World Cup in Qatar by head coach Louis van Gaal.

In his World Cup debut against the United States (3-1), they won in the round of 16 on 3 December 2022.
