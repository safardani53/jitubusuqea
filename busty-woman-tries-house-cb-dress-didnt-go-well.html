Im super busty and tried on the viral House of CB dress - it didnt go well

 Posted on April 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  304 words  |  Martina Birk

IT'S the House of CB dress that has gone viral on social media.

And while the gorgeous floral number makes the waist look tiny and the legs look long, it's not particularly suitable for those with big boobs.

At least, that's what Lucy Dawson found, as she tried on the £139 Tallulah floral puff sleeve midi dress from the British company.

"If you’re like me and you have a larger bust, you have seen these House of CB dresses, like the one I’m wearing, and you’ve wondered, ‘Should I even bother trying that on?’" Lucy began, as she held up a box lid to cover her bust.

"I’ve tried it on for you, I’m gonna reveal."

As she removed the box lid to show the dress in all its glory, she did a big sigh.

"Is it in any way wearable while out and about?" she asked.

"I need opinions because I love the dress, like the construction of the dress is amazing but I just don’t know why they had to do this with the bust area."

As she turned to the side, she showed how tiny the bust area actually was.

"My friends say it looks worse from the side than it does from the front," she said.
