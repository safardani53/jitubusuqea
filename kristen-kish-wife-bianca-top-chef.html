Who is Kristen Kish's wife, Bianca Dusic?

 Posted on March 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  376 words  |  Aldo Pusey

KRISTEN Kish is widely known for her season win during the 10th season of Top Chef.

One person who has always celebrated her success is her wife, Bianca Dusic - and here's everything we know about their relationship.

Who is Kristen Kish?

Kristen Kish is a chef born on December 1, 1983, in Seoul, South Korea.

At four months, Kish was adopted by a family in Michigan where she then grew up.

In 2005, Kish received her associate's degree in culinary arts from Le Cordon Bleu in Chicago, Illinois.

Following her graduation, Kish worked as an instructor for Barbara Lynch's demonstration kitchen, Stir.

In 2012, she was promoted to Chef de Cuisine.

In the same year, Kish competed in Bravo's Top Chef where she defeated Brooke Williamson for the winning title.

Following her Top Chef success, she was a co-host for 36 Hours, and Iron Chef: Quest for an Iron Legend.

In 2023, Kish became the host of National Geographic's Restaurants at the End of the World.

On July 11, Kish was named one of the hosts of season 21 of Top Chef. She'll be replacing the show's previous host, Padma Lakshmi.

Who is Kristen's wife, Bianca Dusic?

Kristen Kish has been married to her wife, Bianca Dusic, since 2021.

Dusic grew up in Australia where she went to college at Deakin University in 2000.

She is now the vice president of food and beverage for Standard Hotels.

Prior to working in her current position, Dusci worked as the director of food and drinks at Soho House.

Additionally, Dusic is also a licensed health coach and certified hypnotherapist.

She currently has her own Instagram page dedicated to her work called b__within.

How long have they been together?

Though Dusic and Kish got married in 2021, the two have been together for quite some time.

It isn't currently known when the two met, however, in 2019, Kish announced the two were engaged on Instagram.

On April 18, 2021, the couple officially tied the knot in an intimate ceremony.
