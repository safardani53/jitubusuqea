JANELLE Brown has been inundated with compliments from fans after she showed off her weight loss at a recent family wedding.

Janelle was in attendance as her son Logan Brown married his longtime girlfriend, Michelle Petty, and she certainly made an effort for the occasion.

The Sister Wives took the opportunity to get glammed up and dressed her slimmer figure in a deep purple floor-length gown.

The flattering dress featured sheer sleeves and a matching neckline as well as a diamanté trim on the collar.

Janelle teamed the dress with a pair of nude-colored shoes and grinned from ear-to-ear as she posed with her family.

When on fan shared the image from Janelle's Instagram Story on Reddit, many quickly commented on her look.

However, other Reddit users were more interested in Kody Brown being missing from the family photos.

Christine is clearly still on good terms with Janelle as the two women cosied up for a photo together.

WEIGHT LOSS JOURNEY

Janelle has been on her weight loss journey for the past few years, and in 2020 she opened up about feeling “confident” in a bathing suit again.

She captioned a selfie from her beach holiday at the time: “For years I made excuses about going anywhere that required swimsuit. All of my insecurities… As I became more accepting of my wonderful body and all the things it could do I became more confident.

“I’m a work in progress for sure but I missed out on so much fun through the years. No more! So here is my yearly post about how to go to the beach. Put on your suit and go," she added.

However, in July of this year some people claimed Janelle was faking her before-and-after weight loss photos.

Some fans accused Janelle of using a filter just so that she could sell diet products.

Reddit fans shared a bunch of photos that the family posted recently, including one of Janelle talking about her weight loss journey.

The photo of the reality TV star showed a side-by-side comparison of her in the same outfit for the past two 4th of July's.

She wore a shirt that resembled the American flag and denim shorts.

In the first photo, Janelle held a Plexus drink, which she heavily promotes often.

In the second photo, she held her shorts out, which she said were "falling off of her."

She captioned the post: " But in the past year, not only have I increased my strength (almost have a 200 lb barbell squat accomplished you guys!) but in my own unique way I have lost lbs and inches."
