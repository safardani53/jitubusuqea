KARDASHIAN critics are claiming Kylie Jenner's lips look like "hot dogs" in a new TikTok video.

The 24-year-old took to the popular platform to show off her new lip plumping gloss.

In the short clip, the Hulu star is getting all glammed up with help from stylists as her brand's gloss is applied to her lips.

Later on, Kylie poses in a sleek, black form-fitting dress with a plunging neckline and smiles for the camera.

However, some viewers were more focused on the star's lips as they were quick to take to an online thread to react.

One said: "Her lips looks like hotdogs," as another agreed and added, "... the hot dog look gives me the creeps."

"It is so weird that her top lip is bigger than the bottom..." a commenter chimed in and a fourth said, "Those lips are looking utterly ridiculous."

One critic even pointed out the detail: "Her lips are bigger?" while another suggested Kylie is "uncomfortable" in her skin.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum has opened up about changes to her lips in the past.

"I was 15, and I was insecure about my lips," she once said about making the decision to get lip fillers.

She continued: "I had really small lips, and it was, like, one of my first kisses, and a guy was like, 'I didn't think you'd be a good kisser because you have such small lips.'

"I took that really hard. Just when a guy you like says that… I don't know; it just really affected me. I didn't feel desirable or pretty."

The TV notable added: "I would overline my lips with the lip liner to create the illusion of bigger lips, and then, finally, I was like, this lip liner isn't doing it, and ended up getting my lips done. It sticks with you - it just got in there."

While the star has received backlash for her large lips, she once made it clear she is fine with them.

"My pictures, I pout them out a lot. I think big lips are awesome," she told E! News in 2015.

BIG LAB DRAMA

It's not just her lips that have been causing controversy, recently Kylie ruffled feathers when she was accused of breaking "sanitary protocols" after she shared sexy photos in a cosmetics lab.

In the snaps, the TV personality- who let her long hair flow while in the lab- wore a white lab coat over her baggy jeans and sneakers as she snapped a test tube filled with product and a shelf filled with jars of sparkly makeup.

However, cosmetics developer Kevin James Bennett was not happy about the pics and said: "I'm a cosmetic developer and work with cosmetic manufacturers (and their labs) as part of my job.

"I have very short hair and I've NEVER been allowed into the lab or onto the manufacturing floor without a hair net, shoe covers, mask…and disposable GLOVES."

"Kylie is gaslighting her followers into thinking she is creating cosmetics..." he added.

'SHAME ON YOU!'

The mom-of-two was quick to hit back and said: "Kevin – this picture is not taken in a manufacturing facility.

"I would never bypass sanitary protocols and neither would any other celeb or beauty brand owner."
