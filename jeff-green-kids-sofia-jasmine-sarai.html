Jeff Green Kids: Meet Sofia, Jasmine And Sarai Green

 Posted on May 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  517 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Denver Nuggets power forward Jeff Green is a doting father to his three beautiful kids, Sofia, Jasmine, and Sarai Green.

With more than sixteen years of experience as an NBA player, Jeff has played for multiple different teams throughout his career.

Aside from the basketball court, Jeff is a proud family man and lives a married life.

He married Stephanie Hurtado after dating her for more than two years.

Together, they share a loving relationship and prioritize their family’s happiness above all else.

Jeff Green Kids: Sofia, Jasmine & Sarai

Stephanie and Jeff Green are married with three beautiful kids – Sofia Green, Jasmine Green, and Sarai Green.

The couple first met through mutual friends at a Miami club in 2014 and immediately hit it off. 

After exchanging phone numbers that night, they began dating three months later.

Jeff proposed to Stephanie during a romantic summer trip to Greece in 2016. The following year, they welcomed their first child, Sofia Green, in February 2017.

After a few months, the duo tied the knot in a beautiful ceremony on August 9, 2017.

Their second baby girl, Jasmine Green, was born in May 2018, bringing more joy and love into their family.

The pair announced the birth of their third daughter, Sarai Green, on March 1, 2022.

Brief on Jeff Green’s Wife, Stephanie Green

The wife of Jeff Green, Stephanie Green-Hurtado, was born on September 17, 1990, in Miami, Florida.

She received her degree from Florida International University in 2015. According to her website, she is a NASM-certified personal trainer.

She has also written a book, “Dream, Little Girl, Dream,” about her two daughters, Sofia and Jasmie.

The book revolves around her daughters exploring their dreams and imagining what they could be, and accomplishing their dreams, believing that nothing is off-limits.

Jeff Green Parents

Jeff Green was born to Jeffrey Green Sr and Felicia Akingube in Cheverly, Maryland, United States, on August 28, 1986.

He grew up alongside his sister, Mia Green.

However, much details about his parents and sister are currently unknown.

NBA Career Of Jeff Green

The Boston Celtics drafted Jeff Green in the 2007 NBA draft as the fifth overall pick but traded him to the Seattle Supersonics.

Green made his NBA debut against the Denver Nuggets on October 31, 2007, and scored five points.

He played four seasons for the Seattle Supersonics/ Oklahoma City Thunder before a mid-season trade to the Boston Celtics.

The Maryland-born basketball player missed the entire 2011-12 season as he underwent heart surgery. 

Kevin Durant, the former teammate of Green, dedicated his season to him.

Jeff recorded his career-high 43 points playing for the Boston Celtics against the Miami Heat on March 18, 2013.

The power forward played 222 games for the Boston Celtics, starting in 134 and averaging 14.6 points per game in four seasons.

He then had a brief stint with different teams like Memphis Grizzlies, Los Angeles Clippers, Orlando Magic, Cleveland Cavaliers, Washington Wizards, Utah Jazz, Houston Rockets, and Brooklyn Nets.

Currently, he is playing for the Denver Nuggets after signing a two-year contract on August 11, 2021.
