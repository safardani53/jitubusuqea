Is Hallmark Actor Tyler Hynes Married? Plus, Details on His Latest Movie

 Posted on February 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  596 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Here's what you need to know about Hallmark star Tyler Hynes — find out if he's married and what he thinks of his adoring fans.

It’s wedding season, which means Hallmark is releasing a lineup of movies to celebrate. The upcoming film My Boyfriend’s Back: Wedding March 5 follows the story of wedding planner, Annalise, who decides to start her own company. Under pressure to impress investors with an amazing reception, she soon finds out that her ex-boyfriend Brad is the best man.

Will these exes start to rekindle their romance? Well, it is a Hallmark movie after all! One thing fans love watching is the feel-good factor played out in the network’s films. Because, haven’t we all imagined ourselves falling in love in a Hallmark movie? In My Boyfriend's Back, we’re sure actor Tyler Hynes — who plays Brad — will catch your eye. 

Here’s what you need to know about the Hallmark star. 

Is Tyler Hynes married?

Though he may play a handsome bachelor in many of his films, Tyler is reportedly taken. While he does not openly flaunt his relationship on social media, he did mention he had a girlfriend in an October 2018 interview.  

Article continues below advertisement

Revealing that he’s not an avid Twitter user, he said, “My girlfriend opened up my Twitter the other day, and I haven’t been on there in so long. She told me I had so many followers on this Twitter account. I don’t know if I had looked at it since I was on Saving Hope." 

While it is not confirmed if the two are still together, we can still get swept away in Tyler’s movies.

He’s also a director. 

Besides working in front of the camera, Tyler also uses his creativity to direct short films. The 33-year-old has posted his work on social media showcasing his talents. Most recently he posted a clip on Instagram of his short film titled, AFK.

Tyler has a dedicated fanbase — and his nickname for them is adorable. 

If you’ve ever been a part of a celeb fanbase, then you know having a nickname for the group is almost always key. As a play on his last name, Tyler refers to his fans a “hynies.” If you’re a huge Tyler-stan, here’s how you can win a t-shirt from one of his starring roles on a Hallmark movie. And yes, he even promised it would be unwashed. 

“For my hallmark #hynies haha. The last image is of one of the Henley shirts I wore in #wm5. Here are the instructions if you want my smelly shirt," he wrote in a recent Instagram post. "Fans of #juneweddings @hallmarkchannel @tyler_hynes here is a giveaway just for you! Tyler wore this grey Henley in #weddingmarch5 & you can win this very item for yourself autographed by Tyler himself!”

Tyler Hynes loves Hallmark movies (and we do too!):

Besides starring in My Boyfriend’s Back, Tyler has also appeared in a few Hallmark made-for-TV movies, such as, Flip That Romance, Falling for You, and It’s Christmas, Eve, 

"I love working with the Hallmark family of actors,” he told My Devotional Thoughts. "I’ve got Andrew Walker, who’s married to my cousin. Then I’ve got my buddy Marcus {Rosner} who is also loved in the Hallmark world." 

Adding, “They’re all great guys. I’m honored to be a part of the Hallmark community now.” 

Catch My Boyfriend’s Back: Wedding March 5 when it airs on June 8 at 9 p.m. ET on Hallmark Channel.
