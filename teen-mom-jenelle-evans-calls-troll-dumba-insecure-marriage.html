TEEN Mom Jenelle Evans served a savage clap back to a hater who claimed her marriage was "insecure."

The MTV alum enjoyed a "kid-free weekend" with her husband David Eason, despite recently coming clean on their past issues.

Jenelle, 29, posted a TikTok reflecting on the couple's romantic stay-cation, where she revealed that her "hubby had a Valentine's Day surprise" for her.

The pair picked up "Starbucks" before heading to "breakfast at Jimbo's" and a "massage."

The Teen Mom 2 stars spoke about their appointment afterward as they sat in the car before leaving the parking lot.

Jenelle asked her husband: "Well babe how was that?" and he replied: "That was amazing!"

The duo laughed about David being "tense" during the process and feeling "drunk" from the massage.

The ex reality star then thanked her spouse and leaned in for a kiss as they wished each other a "Happy Valentine's Day."

One fan was not convinced at the sincerity of the post, as they hopped in the comments to express disbelief.

"Imagine being secure enough to not stop outside the location and get your phone out and record the name of the place and post it on Instagram," they roasted.

"Then record each little bit of your day to try and prove your marriage is all stars and roses."

Jenelle was not in the mood to let the comment slide, as she clapped back: "Nahhh...it's called a #MiniVlog dumba**."

The TV personalities jaunted off on a "kids-free weekend" yesterday, despite Jenelle's reservations about her husband coming to light recently.

The couple enjoyed alone time as they bar-hopped and shared several cocktails.

Conversely, the star just posted a new Youtube video days ago where she revealed her fear that David would take her daughter away.

After up and moving to Tennessee without his consent, Jenelle worried that her slighted husband would come to retrieve their child Ensley, four.

She intimated: “This behavior from David, we don’t know when it will stop. I need to make him shut up because I’m just getting too stressed out.

"So, I didn’t want him contacting me, I wanted to protect my kids, as well, and wanted to make sure he didn’t pull the same thing as me and come to take Ensley away from me when I wasn’t looking.

"I wanted to do this temporarily to basically show him, like, ‘Look, let’s control ourselves. Let’s be civil. Let’s not lash out. Let’s not post anything crazy. And let’s put some distance between us for a while until we figure things out.’”

Jenelle also shared that her reason for leaving was to allow space in her relationship, after claiming the duo have been "up each other's a** since 2015."

The mom of three made the choice after David shot and killed their family dog, resulting in her termination from the MTV network.
