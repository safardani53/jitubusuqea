Steve Burton’s net worth

Steve Burton is well-known for his roles in several films and television programs. He has primarily been recognized as Jason Morgan in General Hospital. As of October 2022, the 52-year-net old’s worth is estimated to be approximately $3 million, according to CelebrityNetWorth. Although specifics on his assets are not public, his work in the entertainment sector has earned him a lot of money.

Burton made his television debut in 1987 with the fantasy comedy Out of This World and then featured on the NBC serial opera Days of Our Lives. He first appeared on General Hospital as Jason Morgan in 1991 and earned a Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series in 1998.

In 2007, Steve returned to the role of Jason in the first season of General Hospital: Night Shift. Following that, he starred in the science fiction miniseries Taken and the 2001 film The Last Castle. He has also voiced characters in Kingdom Hearts, Kingdom Hearts II, Dirge of Cerberus: Final Fantasy VII, Dissidia Final Fantasy, and other games.

The actor was dismissed for failing to follow covid requirements.

He departed General Hospital in October 2012 and was cast the following year as Dylan McAvoy on The Young and the Restless. He returned to General Hospital in 2017 but was sacked in 2021 for refusing to comply with the Covid-19 vaccination obligation imposed by the company. In April 2022, he joined Days of Our Lives: Beyond Salem.
