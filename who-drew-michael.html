Meet Drew Michael: Army officer-turned-CEO and Louise Redknapp's new man

 Posted on April 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  504 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

LOUISE Redknapp's love life has been a hot topic of conversation, ever since her shock split from Jamie Redknapp.

But things are now looking up for the singer, as she has a new man in her life - Drew Michael.

Who is Drew Michael?

Andrew Michael, who goes by Drew Michael, is a London businessman.

He shot to fame when it was revealed he is Louise Redknapp's new man.

Aged 39 (as of September 2023), he is nine years the singer's junior, as she was born in 1974.

The former public school boy studied at the University of Nottingham, before going to Pangbourne College in Reading, Berkshire.

Was Drew Michael in the Army?'

Drew served in the army, and is a former military officer.

The Daily Mail reported that he worked as a Platoon Commander and a Fire Support Group Commander for the forces.

In 2010, Drew was given a Mention in Despatches for his work with The Rifles as part of the Operational Honours and Awards List, with a total of 130 members of the Armed Forces receiving honours and awards.

What does Drew Michael do now?

Drew now works in the aerospace and defence industry.

He is the Chief Executive Officer at Overwatch Group, also known as Overwatch Aerospace Ltd.

The company supplies strategic war equipment to the Royal Marines and the British Army, which is in stark contrast to Louise's pop career.

When did Drew Michael and Louise Redknapp meet?

Drew and Louise were first pictured together in September 2023.

The pop star was seen leaving London's The Groucho Club in Soho together and walked side by side.

The Sun contacted a representative for Louise for comment.

In early 2023, Louise told The Sun that she wasn't fretting about finding a new man having been single for six years following 19 years of marriage.

Ex Jamie Redknapp, who she shares two sons Charlie and Beau, has since remarried and had a baby with new wife Frida Andersson.

Louise said: “I’m just open to what may happen. I’m not actively out there looking. It’s not my number-one goal in life.

“But, if it goes that way and I met somebody who swept me off my feet and was amazing and kind and great, then I’d be so open to exploring that.

“But no, I’m not downloading apps. Well, not at the minute…

“I’ve put so much into rebuilding my career after a long break. Opportunities don’t just happen because people know who you are.

“And for me, it’s really important to be respected in what I do.
