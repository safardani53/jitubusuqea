A JEWELLERY business owner has opened up about how she turned her small company into one worth six figures - with a little help from none other than Stacey Solomon. 

Lucy, the founder of Tomm Jewellery, started making pieces in her bedroom when she was just 19, as she dreamed of making a living doing what she loved.

Since, she’s gone from strength to strength and has revealed how the company has massively grown and now has a dedicated office and four employees.

What’s more, four years on, the business is worth a whopping six figures - and she’s only 23.

However, there seems to be even brighter things on the horizon, as Loose Women star Stacey recently chose a Tomm Jewellery bracelet for her wedding day.

When former X Factor contestant Stacey, 32, tied the knot with new hubby Joe Swash in an intimate, romantic ceremony, the bride opted to accessorise with a simple but stunning handmade freshwater pearl bracelet.
