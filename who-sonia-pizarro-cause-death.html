Who was Sonia Pizarro and what was her cause of death?

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  329 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

TELEVISION personality Sonia Pizarro passed away in May 2023 at the age of 60.

Here's what we know about the life and death of the Operation Repo star.

Who was Sonia Pizarro?

Sonia Pizarro was a TV star known for her role on the truTV reality series Operation Repo.

She starred on the show in the early 2010s and has also been seen in the films Repo Chick (2009) and Followed (2018).

At this time, additional details on Pizarro's life are unclear because she often stayed out of the spotlight and off social media.

What was Sonia Pizarro's cause of death?

Pizarro's death was first confirmed by her niece, Lyndah Pizarro, on May 4, 2023.

She told TMZ that her aunt died the night prior in her sleep at her Arizona home.

Despite confirming her death, Lyndah did not provide a cause, however, she told the outlet that she had a history of serious health issues.

In 2018, she was hospitalized after suffering a stroke, but that is not thought to have contributed to her sudden death.

Shortly after the death was confirmed, fans took to Twitter to offer their condolences.

"May she rest in peace. I enjoyed watching her on the show," one user wrote.

"I loved her. She was the show!RIP," another added.

Was Sonia Pizarro married?

When Pizarro first stepped into the spotlight, she was known for her marriage to fellow Operation Repo star Froylan Tercero, however, they have since divorced.

At this time, it is not known if she ever remarried.

"She will always have a place in my heart and on my body since I still have her name tattooed on my stomach," Tercero told TMZ after her death.

"Sonia we love you and I will honor you with all the good mementos you gave me, thank you and I love you."

She was survived by her three children, Ruby, Robert, and Froylan Jr., and three grandchildren, Bryant, Brie, and Danica.
