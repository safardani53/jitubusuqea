Irina Shayk ex-boyfriend list: Who has the model dated?

 Posted on September 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  712 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

IRINA Shayk is famously known for gracing the cover of Sports Illustrated and appearing in Victoria's Secret campaigns.

Previously, she has dated Bradley Cooper, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Kanye West.

Who has Irina Shayk dated?

Cristiano Ronaldo

Sometime in 2009, Irina Shayk and Cristiano Ronaldo were introduced to one another during a photoshoot for Armani Exchange's summer 2010 swimwear collection.

In June 2010, it was confirmed Irina and Ronaldo began dating.

On January 20, 2015, the former Real Madrid player announced he and the model parted ways.

"After dating for five years, my relationship with Irina Shayk has come to an end," he said in a statement.

"We believed it would be best for both of us to take this step now. I wish Irina the greatest happiness."

Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson

Following Irina's split from Ronaldo, it was speculated she moved on with Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson.

Irina and The Rock became acquainted with one another while co-starring as Hercules and Megara in the 2014 adventure film Hercules.

The rumors of the two's romance continued to swirl but eventually fizzled out.

Neither Irina nor The Rock have confirmed they entered a relationship.

Just months later, the Fast and the Furious actor went on to date Lauren Hashian.

Meanwhile, the Victoria's Secret model sparked romance rumors with Bradley Cooper.

Bradley Cooper

In April 2015, Irina and Bradley Cooper were seen together in New York City on one of many occasions.

On May 13 of the same year, it was confirmed the pair were dating after they were spotted kissing in London.

On March 21, 2017, the couple welcomed their daughter Lea.

On June 6, 2019, it was announced Irina and Bradley split and the former moved out of their Los Angeles home.

At the time, an insider told The Sun: "Things are cooling off between Bradley and Irina in a big way.

"It’s not looking like there’s much long left for the two of them together.

"They haven’t been good for a few months and Irina decided it would be best if she moved into her own place."

Vito Schnabel

In April 2020, Irina and Vito Schnabel ignited relationship reports after they were seen together a few times in Manhattan.

"Irina has known Vito for years and they've always been friends," a source revealed to E! Online.

"They used to double date when Vito was with Heidi and Irina was with Bradley.

"Vito is a big flirt and Irina is his type. She likes the attention.

"They are neighbors and are spending a lot of time together.

"When Irina's daughter is with Bradley, she has downtime and she likes the company."

The insider added: "It's easy for right now to see Vito and go over to his place. They've been spending all day together and the evenings as well.

"She likes connecting with someone she is comfortable with."

Eventually, the relationship rumors about the supposed pair fizzled out.

Kanye West

In early May 2021, it was revealed Irina supposedly struck up a romance with Kanye West.

The model and the GRAMMY Award winner were seen spending time together in the Provence region of France for the latter's 44th birthday.

Their reunion happened 11 years after Irina appeared in Kanye's Power music video.

Regarding their rumored courtship, an insider told The Sun: "Kanye is looking more like his old self again.

"He's taking things slow with Irina and they are just enjoying each other's company.

"The big smile is back and he's got the spring back in his step again.

"They have a lot in common, both are 'from the streets' in terms of their interests and know a lot of the same people.

"Although it's still early days, she's clearly made an impact and it looks like she is motivating him musically."

The dating speculation came to an end in August 2021 and it was reported Irina and Kanye's fling reached its conclusion.

Tom Brady

On July 24, 2023, Irina was at the center of dating rumors after it was believed she and Tom Brady spent the previous weekend together in Los Angeles.

The Sports Illustrated cover girl and the Tampa Bay Buccaneers alum first met at Joe Nahmad and Madison Headrick's wedding in Sardinia.
