Pamela Anderson is an actress, model, and pop culture icon. See the blonde bombshell’s best photos of all time here.

We love Pamela! Pamela Anderson, born July 1, 1967, is a Canadian-American actress and model, known as an iconic blonde bombshell of the 90s. She rose to fame after appearing on the cover of Hugh Hefner’s iconic men’s magazine in 1989, moving to Los Angeles to pursue a modeling career. Pamela continued her pinup modeling with photo shoots, spreads, and the covers of Rolling Stone, Vogue, GQ, and more.

Pamela made the transition to acting in the early 1990s, and is best known as Casey Jean ‘C.J.’ Parker on the series Baywatch. Pamela also starred in movies like Scary Movie 3, Superhero Movie, and even made an appearance as her Baywatch character in the 2019 film. She made her debut on Broadway in Apr. 2022 as Chicago lead Roxie Hart.

The star has also become known for her personal life. She’s been married to rockers like Tommy Lee and Kid Rock, and has two sons with Tommy. She was also married to Rick Salomon twice!
