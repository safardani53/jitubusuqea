Khloe Kardashian Talks O.J. Simpson Paternity Scandal! WATCH!

 Posted on May 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  238 words  |  Aldo Pusey

By now, you’ve probably heard the rumor that O.J. Simpson is Khloe Kardashian’s father.

Fans of the Kard clan have been hearing the reports for years, and they’ve resurfaced in recent weeks, due to renewed interest in Simpson’s famous 1995 trial.

But despite the way the Kardashians have been portrayed in the People v. O.J. Simpson, in real life, they’ve done their best to distance themselves from the accused double murderer and current convict.

That’s why it’s a bit surprising to see Khloe address (however briefly) the rumor that she’s the result of an affair between Kris Jenner and Simpson.

In the clip below, a guest on her FYI talk show, Kocktails With Khloe, asks the the 31-year-old how she responds to some of the more pernicious rumors that have circulated about her over the years.

Khloe cites the story about Kris Jenner’s affair as one of the most painful, not because it calls her paternity into question, but because it disparages her late father, who’s not around to defend himself.

“She wanted to come out 10 years after my dad passed away and say I’m not my dad’s daughter," Khloe said. "And he confided in her. I was like you can talk about me and my sisters all you f**king want. Do not talk about my dad.”

Kris has admitted to cheating on Robert, Sr., but clearly Khloe roundly rejects the notion that she might not be her father’s daughter.
