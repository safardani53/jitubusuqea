Leo Mezie Death

The death of popular Nollywood actor Leo Mezie has sparked widespread grief. Leo died on May 14, 2022. Chioma Toplis, one of his known friends, informed him of his death. Chioma shared a photo of Leo on Instagram with the caption “Leo’s death news with the Nigerian people.” She had the same cause of death as Leo. Chioma revealed that Leo died as a result of post-kidney transplant complications.

He had a kidney transplant last week, but after a week, he developed severe health complications and died in the hospital. Leo had his first kidney transplant four years ago, and this was his second. He had been suffering from malaria, typhoid, and pneumonia since last year. A high dose of medication used to treat diseases such as malaria damaged his kidney, prompting him to seek a second kidney transplant.
