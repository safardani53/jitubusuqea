CITIBANK is set to shut down three branches this month and customers might be left with a tough decision to switch banks.

The closures are all set to happen in California.

Here are the Citibank addresses affected:

  • 10460 Mount Gleason Avenue, Tujunga
  • 41969 Big Bear Boulevard, Big Bear Lake
  • 1995 41st Avenue, Capitola

It's unclear when exactly they will close – but they are all set to shutter sometime this month.

That means customers who prefer going to physical branches might have to make a tough call if there are no more Citibank's near them.

The only options may be to continue to bank online or switch to a rival that's nearby.

You can use Citibank's online tool to find the nearest branch near you.

Also, California will be saying goodbye to several other major banks in September.

This includes Bank of America, Wells Fargo, JP Morgan, and Flagstar Bank.

In total 10 banks will close in California this month.

That was followed by 16 shutters in August.

Citibank has more than 700 branches in the US.

MORE MAJOR BANK SHUTTERS

But California isn't the only state that has been losing banks.

Wells Fargo is closing a location in Allentown, Pennsylvania as consumers have been shifting their banking habits.

"This is not an easy decision or one we take lightly," a company spokesperson told The U.S. Sun in a statement.

"Branches continue to play an important role in the way we serve our customers, and we continuously evaluate our branch network in light of changing customer needs, the increase in the use of digital banking, and market factors."

The bank will close on November 1.

In total, Wells Fargo is closing 37 branches this year alone.

Regions Bank will close three branches in Chicago, Illinois; Kansas City, Missouri; and Cincinnati, Ohio, according to a report from National Mortgage News.

Those locations only offer mortgage services and are outside of the bank's "traditional branch network footprint."

The locations are set to close during the middle of October.

Associated Bank will also be saying goodbye to six locations in Wisconsin and Illinois.

The addresses and cities impacted are as follows:

  • 2815 South Chicago Avenue in South Milwaukee, Wisconsin
  • 300 North LaSalle Drive in Chicago, Illinois
  • 5987 County Highway West in Manitowish Waters, Wisconsin
  • 3847 South Howell Avenue in Milwaukee, Wisconsin
  • 2001 South Webster Avenue in Allouez, Wisconsin
  • 717 Main Street in Menomonie, Wisconsin

After those branches shutter, there will be 196 locations left.
