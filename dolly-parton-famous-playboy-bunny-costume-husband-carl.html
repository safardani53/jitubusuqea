DOLLY Parton has posted a photo of herself harkening back to her Playboy days.

The iconic country singer posted the pic to Instagram, along with T-shirts fans could buy with her Playboy cover on the front.

In the photo, the 77-year-old wore the exact same outfit she wore on the cover of Playboy in 1978.

The star looked incredible in the black strapless bodysuit with a sparkly mesh torso covering, teamed with full-length black gloves.

Dolly also sported the original big pink bow choker, and wore the famous Playboy bunny ears.

She naturally opted for full glam to complete the ensemble, with sweeping lashes and glittery eyeshadow, while her hair was piled up into a glamorous updo.

The pic showed Dolly as she happily presented a frame to her rarely-seen husband, Carl Thomas Dean.

In the frame was the star's original cover from over 40 years ago, along with a contemporary photo of the singer-songwriter in the getup today.

She captioned the image: "Any bunny think Carl will like this new shirt?"

BUNNY GIRL

Dolly wore the look last year for Carl's 80th birthday.

She explained on Instagram at the time: “You’re probably wondering why I’m dressed like this. Well, it’s for my husband’s birthday.

“Remember some time back I said I was gonna pose on Playboy Magazine when I was 75? Well, I’m 75 and they don’t have a magazine anymore, but my husband always loved the original cover of Playboy, so I was trying to think of something to do to make him happy.

“He still thinks I’m a hot chick after 57 years and I’m not gonna try to talk him out of that. And I hope he agrees. What do you think?”

After laughing, Dolly explained that she “did a little photo shoot in this little outfit” for Carl’s birthday.

She added: “I had a cover made of the new Dolly- or the old new Dolly.”

The hitmaker then explained her plans to surprise Carl by singing to him in her “little bunny suit,” adding: “What do you think? Do you think Playboy should pay me for ought to pay me for this photoshoot? I do.”
