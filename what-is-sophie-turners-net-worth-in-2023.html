Who is Sophie Turner?

Sophie Turner is a British actress known for her character Sansa Stark in HBO's iconic series Game Of Thrones.

The talented star made her Hollywood debut with psychological thriller Another Me in 2013.

She also appeared in the X-Men movie series and in Hollywood’s Barely Lethal.

But it was her role in Game Of Thrones that catapulted her to stardom landing a Primetime Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series.

What is Sophie Turner's net worth?

Sophie seems to have scooped a big pile of cash from her successful acting career throughout the years.

The award-winning actress' net worth continually grew since she stepped onto the Hollywood red carped.

Her role in the fantasy series Game of Thrones went on from 2011 to 2019 - featuring in a whopping 59 episodes.

It is reported that Sophie made over $175,000 per episode but she revealed the pay was less compared to the rest of the cast.

She told Harper's Bazaar: “Kit got more money than me, but he had a bigger storyline.

"And for the last series, he had something crazy like 70 night shoots, and I didn’t have that many. I was like, ‘You know what…you keep that money.'”

She was also cast for X-Men films playing the role of Jean Gray in X-Men: Apocalypse and X-Men: Dark Phoenix in 2019

Turner also portrayed Margaret Ratliff in eight episodes of the 2022 Netflix miniseries The Staircase.

In 2022, she starred in the films Survive and Broken Soldier. 

Beyond her long list of roles, the British star appeared in many magazine covers including Elle, the Cut, Harper's Bazaar and Vogue China in 2019.

Beauty brand deals and fashion partnerships also add to her long list of economic gains.

According to various reports, Sophie has an estimated net worth of $12 million.
