1. Zoe Boyle’s Short Wiki-Bio & Age

A new year is a new beginning for a new journey in life; it was the same for the ballerina mother and barrister father of Zoe Boyle who was born on 1 January 1989 in England.

The talented actress, who was of age 17 then, graduated from the Royal Central School of Speech & Drama, at the University of London in 2006.

Zoe’s interest in acting started at an early age of eight when an ambitious teacher cast her as Hamlet – a truncated version of Hamlet. After that, her interest in period dramas grew, and when she watched Pride And Prejudice on TV, she really wanted to be in something like that; all the costumes and the houses.

But her mother always tried to talk her out of choosing acting as her career because of the hardships while her father motivated her to follow her dreams.

 2. Zoe Boyle’s Career Progression As An Actress

After graduation following her dreams, she grabbed her earliest screen roles as the guest spots in Agatha Christie's Poirot and Great Performances in 2008. Her first job out of drama school was in an RSC production of King Lear, with Ian McKellen as the lead.
