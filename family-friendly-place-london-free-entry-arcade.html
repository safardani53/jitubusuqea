A MUM has revealed she’s discovered the best place in London to entertain the kids for hours without breaking the bank. 

Tameka Milian loves to go around the UK to experience kid-friendly activities and visit places to enjoy with her little one. 

And in one video, she explained that she had been invited to go to Babylon Park, which is located in Camden. 

Taking to her social media page, she told viewers: “It’s an indoor theme park that has free entry and fun for the whole family.” 

Panning the camera over the interior, viewers could see roller coasters, arcade games and a soft play area. 

“Their food is also halal, which really caters for everyone,” she continued.

She then went to a ticket machine to top up her card balance in a bid to enjoy some of the games on offer in the park. 

Tameka said: “It runs completely cashless as you have to top up the card or the app, which you use to play the activities. 

“This allows you to control your spending and I just love the no time limit, so you can stay there for as long as you want - making it a real family day out.” 

The doting mum said her own son loved it, noting that the soft play area was his favourite part.

In the caption, she added: “Am ust visit indoor theme park for the whole family. Children will absolutely love it.

“We were invited to spend the day here and enjoyed it so much. Would definitely recommend taking the kids, we will be back soon.” 

And it appears people agreed with her assessment as one person commented: “I was there this afternoon and it was fantastic. My son loved it!” 

A second shared: “You won me when you said halal food,” to which she replied: “Love that. Everywhere should serve it, let’s be honest it’s 2023!” 

While a third asked: “Looks great where is this place? And do you know prices? Thank you”, 

TikTok user @temaka.milian replied: “Name is always at the end, Babylon Park, NW1 8QU. Prices vary, depending how much you want to top up. I believe the cheapest package is £15 x”. 

Babylon Park has a roller coaster for those aged 4 and up, a drop tower, bumper cars, an alien-themed ride for toddlers, a kiddie wheel, a soft play area, and vending machines. 

While you can buy tickets there, you can also purchase coins online. The cheapest package is £15, which gives you 144 coins. 

You can also buy packages of 240 coins, 480 and 24000 that’s perfect for those ready to spend the entirety of the day there. 

People have raved about the place on TripAdvisor as one person shared: “This place is mostly an arcade with some small rides/bumper car type things for kids. 

“What I really like is that you get a card instead of dealing with coins/tokens and the card also stores all the tickets you earn.”
