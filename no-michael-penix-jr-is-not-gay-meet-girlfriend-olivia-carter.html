No, Michael Penix Jr Is Not Gay: Meet Girlfriend Olivia Carter

 Posted on April 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  572 words  |  Martina Birk

Contrary to rumors, football quarterback Michael Penix Jr. isn’t gay; in fact, he has a girlfriend.

The footballer is in a relationship with Olivia Carter, an athlete like him.

Explore further to discover the intriguing details of his girlfriend and the dynamics of their relationship.

Michael Penix Jr graduated from Tampa Bay Technical High School, where he was a quarterback, throwing 61 touchdowns and just six interceptions in 24 games as a starter.

Commencing all 13 games in his debut year at UW, he concluded the 2022 season as the NCAA-FBS leader in passing yards per game.

 Additionally, he secured the third spot in completions per game in the final standings and is the second Husky ever to eclipse 4,000 yards in a season.

Moreover, he won the Maxwell Award for the nation’s outstanding player and secured the highest-ever second place in Heisman Trophy voting for a Husky.

Is Michael Jr Gay? Who Is His Girlfriend?

No, the American football quarterback is not gay and is, in fact, in a relationship with Olivia Carter.

Olivia is the daughter of Christopher and Regina Carter and has an older brother, Quincy Carter, and a younger brother, Theo Carter.

Having completed her education at Kent-Meridian High School in Kent, Wash, Olivia graduated in 2018.

Furthermore, she earned athletic letters throughout all four years in soccer and track and two years in basketball.

Additionally, she received first-team all-league honors for soccer each of the four years and was also recognized as first-team all-league in track.

Moreover, she participated as a player in the ODP Region IV pool and achieved the status of a semi-finalist in the USYS Regional IV Championship in 2017.

In her freshman year, she featured in all 19 matches, making 16 starts and accumulating a total of 758 minutes in the forward position.

During her junior year with the Wolf Pack, she played in seven matches, starting six and recording an assist in a draw against San Jose State, totaling 417 minutes in the forward position.

Finally, in her senior year, she played in 16 games, and started 11, tallying 1,028 minutes and attempting one shot against Saint Mary’s.

Apart from football, Olivia finds pleasure in engaging in workouts, watching movies, and spending quality time with friends and family.

Michael Penix Jr Gay | Michael And Olivia Relationship

While many athletes prefer keeping their relationships private, Olivia and Michael seem to enjoy sharing their love on social media.

While the exact timeline of their, relationship is undisclosed, the two seem to be in a very serious and loving relationship.

In Olivia Carter, Washington Huskies quarterback Michael Penix Jr. seems to have discovered his biggest supporter.

Penix’s girlfriend has been accompanying him and his team, the Huskies, offering her steadfast support both online and offline.

Before the Huskies’ national championship clash against Michigan, Carter shared an Instagram story, enjoying drinks with a friend at a Huskies event party, captioned “Cheers, Go Dawgs.”

She also attended the Sugar Bowl, witnessing the Huskies’ triumphant 37-31 victory against the No. 4 Texas Longhorns.

While Washington Huskies quarterback Michael Penix missed the 2023 Heisman, his girlfriend made sure he didn’t feel small.

Carter openly expressed her emotions in her Instagram story, showering love and admiration for Penix, regardless of the outcome.

On Christmas of 2023, Olivia posted a sequence of pictures on Instagram featuring her boyfriend, accompanied by the caption, “All I want for Christmas is You.”
