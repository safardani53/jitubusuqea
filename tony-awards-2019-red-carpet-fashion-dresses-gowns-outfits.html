Tony Awards 2019 Red Carpet Fashion: Dresses, Gowns, Outfits

 Posted on March 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  234 words  |  Aldo Pusey

If anyone knows drama, it’s theater stars, which is why it’s no surprise the fashion at the 2019 Tony Awards held on Sunday, June 9, at Radio City Music Hall in New York City was fierce. From trains to sequins to capedresses, the ladies brought it all to the Tonys red carpet and we can’t help but give them all a standing ovation.

Of course, the dresses and outfits weren’t the only big winners of the night. Shows including Hadestown, The Cher Show and Ink all took home statues. But the fashion truly wowed. A few of the stand outs: Rachel Brosnahan stunned in blue, Kristin Chenoweth went mini but mighty thanks to her bold sleeves and Sienna Miller caught attention with a very sparkly belt buckle.

But it was Thalia Sodi who went for, oh, just about everything at once with a single statement sleeve, feathers and sequins all featured on one show-stopper of a gown. You know the saying “go big or go home”? Well, Sodi needs a housesitter because she never has to leave a party with this outfit.
