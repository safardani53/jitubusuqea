    How heavy should my umbrella base be?

    First things first, think about the weather where your umbrella base is going to be, well, based. If it's prone to wind then we obviously recommend opting for one of the heavier designs.

    However, in general, quality weighted umbrella bases should weigh in at 25kg upwards, to ensure that your umbrella doesn't run away with the wind.

    Some designs weigh over 100kg when filled with water or sand, which is unbeatable for all-year-round use in the garden.
