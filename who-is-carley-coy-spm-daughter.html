CARLEY Coy is the daughter of jailed rapper and sexual offender Carlos Coy aka SPM.

Carley has run her father's record label since he was handed a 45 year prison sentence in 2002 for aggravated sexual assault of a nine-year-old. But who is Carley Coy and who is SPM's wife?

Who is rapper SPM's daughter Carley Coy?

Carley took over the Dope House Records label years after her father was sent down.

She has since set up a merch store at Corpus Christi Trade centre in Houston, Texas, flogging her dad's memorabilia.

Carley also makes her own music including the album My Father’s Revenge, which was released in 2017.

Prior to that Carley released EP Daughter Of The S.O.N. (D.O.T.S) in 2016, and in 2019 she released a four CD EP Collection featuring tracks from My Father's Revenge and D.O.T.S, as well as The Forgotten Folders and Dope World Entertainment Family Reunion.

She has her own YouTube channel called At The House.

Carley shot back into the spotlight in July 2021 when rumors swirled that her sexual offender dad had died in his cell in Texas.

Carley confirmed to her 4,875 Instagram followers that SPM was not dead.

In an Instagram Story, she wrote: “SPM is alive. For everyone in my DM’s, don’t believe the rumors.”

She then revealed that she had spoken to her dad on the phone earlier that day, confirming that the death hoax was fake.

It is unclear how old Carley is and whether she has her own children.

Who is SPM's wife?

Carolyn Rodriguez Coy is a singer with a big social media following.

She has had over 9.3 million views on her YouTube channel.

She appears to perform under DOPEGIRL MUSIC.

Videos show her at low rider car events in Austin, Texas.
